<div class="about-bio">

<p>Cybersecurity specialist with a passion for both sides of the coin — breaking things to understand how to defend them.</p>

<p>I work on threat hunting, detection engineering, and security solution development. As a Subject Matter Expert in the Elastic Stack, I design and build scalable SIEM detection pipelines that turn raw telemetry into actionable alerts. On the offensive side, I focus on Active Directory attacks, red teaming, and web exploitation.</p>

<p>With a solid programming foundation built through years of education and practice, I approach challenges analytically and enjoy building efficient, elegant technical solutions. I'm constantly learning and exploring new techniques — and after hours, you'll usually find me on <a href="https://app.hackthebox.com/users/341478" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Hack The Box</a>, chasing flags and sharpening my skills.</p>

<p>I've always wanted to be a hacker… though most days I still feel like a script kiddie 😉</p>
